About Us

The Foundation is a data clearinghouse about the good life. We collect and distribute data about what kinds of activities people feel good about after the fact, versus which activities people tend to regret.

We also make available software and demo code for making recommendation systems that take this data into account.

In the future we hope to administer a "Time Well Spent" label that will appear across the web and apps, to help users everywhere choose options they are unlikely to regret.

Founded by Joe Edelman (@jxe) in 2013.
